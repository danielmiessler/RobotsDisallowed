		<div class="fl-rich-text">
	<p>If you are an Anthem Blue Cross Blue Shield subscriber, you may have been affected by the recent change of status of Hartford HealthCare (HHC). HHC is now considered out-of-network for Anthem subscribers. This means that if you stay with your current HHC provider, you may incur large out-of-pocket expenses.</p>
<p>UConn Health is still in-network for Anthem customers, and we are happy to assist you during this time of transition. Please call our Help Hotline at <strong>(860) 679-3199</strong>, and our specialists will help you choose new providers for all of your healthcare needs.</p>
