<p><b>Mabinogi World Wiki</b> is a dedicated English database of Mabinogi, an MMO developed by devCAT Studio and distributed by Nexon North America.
</p><p>The wiki is currently being maintained by various contributors and editors like you. First established in December of 2007, we have <a href="/view/Special:Statistics" title="Special:Statistics"><b>13,500</b></a> articles to date. 
</p><p>If you would like to help out, please see <a href="/view/Wiki_Tips_for_New_Editors" title="Wiki Tips for New Editors">Wiki Tips for New Editors</a>. Use the discussion tab on this page to propose new projects or changes or to ask for formatting help. Lastly, please refer to the <a href="/view/Mabinogi_World_Wiki:Policy" title="Mabinogi World Wiki:Policy">Wiki Policy</a>, or visit our <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="//wiki.mabinogiworld.com/forums">Forum</a> (webchat: <a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="http://webchat.ppirc.net/?channels=mabinogi">[1]</a>) for general guidance.
