<h2>Overview</h2>



        <p>Consumers demand choice and convenience when it comes to paying their bills. BillMatrix Payments from Fiserv makes it easy to give your customers secure, convenient payment options while balancing the cost of doing so. We offer a fully outsourced solution that supports bill payments using all major credit cards, signature debit cards, PIN-less debit/ATM cards and electronic check/ACH. </p>
        <p>Our flexible payment choices for customers mean faster payments and fewer collections problems for your business. With BillMatrix Payments, your customers can make one-time payments that are delivered in real-time or schedule their payments in advance. </p>
        <p>With BillMatrix Payments, your customer information is securely stored and transmitted in full compliance with all credit card associations, ATM debit networks, NACHA and federal banking requirements. We take care of things like PCI compliance and SSAE 16 audits so you don't have to. </p>
